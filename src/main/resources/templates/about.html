About this place

I have created this place with idea to let people able to see some cute animals or let them show their beloved pets.
There are no constraints about how many pictures You can link to specific animal or how many pets You can register.
This place is to spread love not war. The whole idea was to make people forget about their problems for a bit and make them smile because... come on everybody loves little cute creatures.

Of course there is a plenty of work still and soon there will be more cool thinks like giving likes to a picture, get some useful tips from other pet owners who can help you with teaching your beloved animal some tricks and many more.

I can only ask people to be mature enough to follow some simple rules.
1 - DON`T UPLOAD UNAPPROPRIATED PICTURES !
2 - DON`T COMMENT THINKS THAT YOU WON`T LIKE TO SEE ON YOUR PICTURES !
